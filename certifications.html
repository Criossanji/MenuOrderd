Our Certifications

Croissanji operates under Acacia S.A.R.L., a fully registered and certified business in Lebanon.

Intellectual Property Registration

The Croissanji brand and logo are officially registered with Lebanon's Intellectual Property Office, protecting our unique identity and trademark.

Company Registration — Ministry of Finance

Acacia S.A.R.L. is registered with the Lebanese Ministry of Finance, ensuring full compliance with financial and tax regulations.

Commercial Registration — Ministry of Justice

Acacia S.A.R.L. is registered at the Mount Lebanon Commercial Register under the Ministry of Justice as a legally established commercial entity.
