About Us

Croissanji is a food concept developed under Acacia.

It reimagines croissant dough by turning it into unique savory creations. With a focus on quality ingredients and consistency, Croissanji offers a selection of snacks, sandwiches, and burgers made to deliver a memorable experience.

Built on creativity and care, the brand continues to grow while earning customer trust with every bite.
